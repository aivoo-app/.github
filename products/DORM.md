## ✨ Core Features

### 🛏️ Intelligent Reservation Management
Easily manage room bookings and availability while maintaining detailed guest profiles. The system synchronizes with various online travel agencies (OTAs) to maximize visibility and prevent overbooking.

### 🛎️ Streamlined Front Desk & Billing
Simplify check-in/out processes and handle complex billing with automated invoicing. Integrated payment gateways ensure secure and efficient transactions for every guest.

### 🧹 Housekeeping & Inventory Coordination
Schedule and track housekeeping tasks in real-time to ensure rooms are always guest-ready. Monitor essential supplies like linens and toiletries to prevent shortages through integrated inventory management.

### 📱 Guest Self-Service Portal
Empower guests to manage their own stay. Through the portal, guests can view billing, update reservations, and communicate directly with staff, reducing front-desk congestion.

### 📊 Advanced Analytics & Reporting
Generate custom reports on occupancy trends, revenue metrics, and guest feedback. Make data-driven decisions to optimize your hotel's performance and service quality.
